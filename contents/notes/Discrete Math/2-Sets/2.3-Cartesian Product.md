## Tuple/Ordered Pairs
A *tuple* or *ordered pairs* is an ordered collection of 
objects or elements

## Cartesian Product
For two sets *A* and *B*: A cartesian product is an operator 
that operates on two sets where it contains all possible ordered 
pairs from *A* to *B*.
