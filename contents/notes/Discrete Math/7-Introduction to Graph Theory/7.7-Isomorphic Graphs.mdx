## Isomorphic Graphs
For two graphs *G* and *H*: these two are *isomorphic* iff. 

1. $$\Phi : V(G) \to V(H)$$
2. For two adjacent vertices *u* and *v* in *G*, $\Phi(u)$ and $\Phi(v)$
   must also be adjacent in *H*.

## Isomorphism
A function that preserves the mapping of a structure.
