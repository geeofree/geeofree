## Network Interface Cards (NICs)
A network port which can be installed or embedded.

## Switch
A computer device that connects end-systems with other 
end-systems in a network.

## Router
A computer device that connects networks with other 
networks.

## Modem (Modulator/Demodulator)
A computer device that transforms digital signals into 
analog signals and vice versa.
